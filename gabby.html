About Me

I'm Louize Gabrielle B. Calleja, a proud Philippine Science High School (Pisay) scholar.

Of course there's a side of me that needs a break from academic stress. That's where swimming comes in. It's my other world, my happy place where I can clear my mind. The pool is where I go to get rid of stress, have fun, and recharge. It's the perfect balance to my school life, and it's something I'll always love.

My Swimming Journey

I started swimming when I was 8 years old. At first, it was difficult, I even cried on the first day but eventually got over it and finished my lessons. After that, I kept doing more until I leveled up into competitive swimming, started joining competitions, and training every afternoon.

Trainings

I usually practice 2-3 times per week depending on whether I don't have any schoolworks.
